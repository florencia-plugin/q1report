Rock 1 — Improve Retention & Onboarding Conversions

In Progress

Rather than rushing to build, this quarter focused on researching the right delivery format for the SEO Academy before committing to development. The Academy is a structured education layer — blog articles, in-app videos, knowledge base articles, Fin responses — designed to help customers understand and act on their SEO issues so they stay and get real value from the app. Three delivery approaches were evaluated and documented before picking a direction.

SEO Academy — 3 Delivery Approaches Evaluated

Approach 1 — Videos & Blog Posts

Not the primary solution
ProsCons
Visual — better than text for explaining complex UI steps Duplicates what's already in help articles — little added value
In-app accessible — users don't need to leave the tool Static and costly — breaks whenever the UI changes
No personalization — same content for every user regardless of their store's actual issues

Approach 2 — Intercom Fin with Contextual Buttons

Not the primary solution
ProsCons
Custom entry points — opening message triggered by a specific button (e.g. "help with meta tags") Cannot read or analyze URLs — a dealbreaker for an SEO tool
Can generate examples if the user provides a product title Complex setup: tags → workflows → snippets → coded buttons in app
No way to give Fin different instructions per button; relies on one global knowledge base

Approach 3 — Custom AI Agent (Internal Build)

Most promising — alignment needed
ProsCons
Can be programmed with specific SEO logic (length, keywords, accessibility rules) Higher initial development and integration effort
Can read and analyze a customer's page URL in real time — unlike Fin
True SEO expertise, not general-purpose AI responses

We need to decide which path to take and make sure the whole team is aligned and walking in the same direction before we build. Andrii has an internal testing environment ready to evaluate approaches safely before shipping.

Rock A

Implement AI in Customer Retention

  • Offer Claude skills for our customers

Rock B

Optimize pluginuseful.com

  • Add BFS badge across the site
  • Add listicles
  • Core copywriting gap: Features tell. Benefits sell. But transformations convert. Current copy is feature-descriptive, not transformation-focused — it tells you what the apps do but doesn't make you feel the urgency of why you need them right now. The emotional hook ("more customers finding your store") drives action. A feature list rarely does.
  • Offer an SEO Skill as a lead capture
  • Add FAQ section and FAQ schema markup
  • The homepage has no statistics, no citations, no data — AI engines won't cite a page with no factual anchors. Princeton GEO research shows statistics addition improves AI citation visibility by 25.9%.
  • Add "Supported by a real expert team" — social proof and team credibility signals
  • Add before/after section showing results with QSS or our apps
  • Use Claude skills workflow to produce SEO articles for pluginuseful.com (target: 1/week) — e.g. Shopify SEO Benchmark Report 2026
  • Add LLMS.TXT file for AI crawler visibility
  • Check Ahrefs blog for content trends and keyword opportunities
Rock C

Bundle Plans & Expansions

  • In-app banner for QSS promotion

  • In SEO issue cards, add a "Fix with AI" button that offers a free AI plan trial
  • Add "Hire an Expert" button
