Rock 1 — Improve Retention & Onboarding Conversions

In Progress

Rather than rushing to build, this quarter focused on researching the right delivery format for the SEO Academy before committing to development. Three delivery approaches were evaluated and documented before picking a direction.

SEO Academy — 3 Delivery Approaches Evaluated

Approach 1 — Videos & Blog Posts

Not the primary solution
ProsCons
Visual — better than text for explaining complex UI steps Duplicates what's already in help articles — little added value
In-app accessible — users don't need to leave the tool Static and costly — breaks whenever the UI changes
No personalization — same content for every user regardless of their store's actual issues

Approach 2 — Intercom Fin with Contextual Buttons

Not the primary solution
ProsCons
Custom entry points — opening message triggered by a specific button Cannot read or analyze URLs — a dealbreaker for an SEO tool
Can generate examples if the user provides a product title Complex setup: tags → workflows → snippets → coded buttons in app
No way to give Fin different instructions per button

Approach 3 — Custom AI Agent (Internal Build)

Most promising — alignment needed
ProsCons
Can be programmed with specific SEO logic (length, keywords, accessibility rules) Higher initial development and integration effort
Can read and analyze a customer's page URL in real time — unlike Fin
True SEO expertise, not general-purpose AI responses

We need team alignment on direction before building. Andrii has an internal testing environment ready.

🚀 Q2 Plan & Proposed Rocks

Q2 2026 Plan

With both Plugin AI and Plugin SEO BFS certified, the company shifts fully to retention in Q2. My work follows the same direction: reactivate what was paused, launch what was built, and bring AI into the customer journey in a targeted way.

Proposed Q2 Rocks

To be confirmed during the Q2 planning session.

Rock A

Implement AI in Customer Retention

  • Offer Claude skills for our customers
  • Add reports to QSS made with AI
  • Design and test an automated SEO health report for premium plan users
Rock B

Optimize pluginuseful.com

  • Add BFS badge across the site
  • Add listicles
  • Core copywriting gap: Features tell. Benefits sell. But transformations convert. Current copy is feature-descriptive — it tells you what the apps do but doesn't make you feel the urgency of why you need them right now.
  • Offer an SEO Skill as a lead capture
  • Add FAQ section and FAQ schema markup
  • The homepage has no statistics, no citations, no data — AI engines won't cite a page with no factual anchors. Princeton GEO research shows statistics addition improves AI citation visibility by 25.9%.
  • Add "Supported by a real expert team" — social proof and team credibility signals
  • Add before/after section showing results with QSS or our apps
  • Use Claude skills workflow to produce SEO articles for pluginuseful.com (target: 1/week)
  • Add LLMS.TXT file for AI crawler visibility
  • Check Ahrefs blog for content trends and keyword opportunities
Rock C

Bundle Plans & Expansions

  • In-app banner for QSS promotion
  • Offer an annual plan option at 15–20% discount ($24/mo billed annually) — annual subscribers churn 60–80% less in SaaS benchmarks
  • Add visible usage quota for the AI listings free tier: e.g. "5 of 20 free AI generations used this month"
  • In SEO issue cards, add a "Fix with AI" button that offers a free AI plan trial
  • Add "Hire an Expert" button
Rock D

New Onboarding

  • Redesign onboarding: Intercom banner for the welcome call prompt, native in-app product tour to guide users through setup
  • Rethink Intercom outbound messages for new users
Rock E

Run the CSM Function

  • Host welcome calls
  • Sell and deliver QSS (Quick Start Service)
  • Refine QSS delivery templates and best practices after each engagement
  • Maintain timely response to customer inquiries across Plugin SEO and Plugin AI
  • Represent customers in weekly team calls — bring support trends and friction points to the table
  • Document and share top recurring issues with Andrii and Miguel on an ongoing basis
  • New FAQs and support articles to address common new concerns
